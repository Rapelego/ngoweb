About Bright Futures Aftercare

Our Story

Bright Futures Aftercare is a non-profit organisation dedicated to supporting children aged 7–13 with their academic, social, and personal development. Founded in 2022, our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can thrive after school hours.

What We Offer

  • Homework Assistance – Tutors and volunteers help children complete schoolwork effectively.
  • Reading Circles – Activities to improve literacy and foster a love for books.
  • Sports & Recreation – Games and activities that build teamwork and health.
  • Nutritious Meals – Wholesome food to keep children energised and cared for.

Our Mission

We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to grow, learn, and shine. Bright Futures Aftercare provides the support and care children need to develop into confident, compassionate, and capable individuals.

Join us in making a difference — whether by enrolling your child, volunteering your time, or supporting us through donations, you can help create brighter futures.
